About Me

I'm a third year Ph.D. student in the School of Cyber Security & Privacy and School of Electrical & Computer Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology. I am advised by Professor Brendan Saltaformaggio and I work in the Cyber Forensics Innovation (CyFI) Lab.

My research interest lies in developing forensic techniques to investigate malware abuse and building systems to prevent those abuse. I focus on the Android ecosystem, and my areas of research span across accessibility security, large-scale malware analysis, forensic techniques, privacy leakage discovery, and system design that secure user privacy.
